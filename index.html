About US

The primary objective of the zeroWaste app is to address the pervasive problem of household food waste by providing users with practical tools and resources to minimize wastage and promote sustainable consumption habits. With the understanding that a significant portion of food waste occurs at the household level, our aim is to empower individuals to take proactive steps towards reducing their environmental footprint and contributing to broader sustainability efforts.

Motivated by alarming food waste statistics and its environmental impact, the zeroWaste team is dedicated to creating a user-friendly solution. By leveraging mobile technology, we aim to make food waste reduction accessible and straightforward for everyone. Our goal is to empower users from all backgrounds to join the movement toward a more sustainable future, reducing landfill waste and mitigating methane emissions.
